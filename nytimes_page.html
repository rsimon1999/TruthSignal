The 30 Best Personalized Gifts

Updated October 17, 2025

By Mari Uyehara and Samantha Schoech

FYI

Just in time for the holidays, we’ve added a few new custom gifts, including a rocks glass sporting a map of a meaningful locale and a custom house portrait.

October 2025

The thrill of seeing one’s name emblazoned on something isn’t reserved just for 7-year-olds scoring mini license plates — everyone feels special unwrapping a gift created specifically for them. In that spirit, we’ve collected our favorite personalized gifts; many, like the bespoke Converse shoes and the personalized Yeti tumbler, are customizable versions of Wirecutter picks. Whether you want to have a gift monogrammed, custom-made, or personalized in another way, these gifts will show the recipient that you were truly thinking of them. Note that most personalized gifts take a little longer to ship.

Looking for other particularly personal gifts? Check out our guides to the best care packages, the best photo gifts, and our favorite gift baskets.

No place like home

MyDaVinci Custom House Portraits

These decidedly non-AI-generated artworks are quick, accurate, and eminently charming. The house portraits are particularly successful.

Buy from myDaVinci

More than survival structures, houses are often the places where people build memories, raise children into adults, and tighten family bonds. You can honor your loved ones’ special place simply and powerfully with MyDaVinci Custom House Portraits. Just submit a high-res image of the house to MyDaVinci and choose an artistic medium (watercolor, pencil sketch, oil painting) that would suit your giftee. In a few days, you’ll get a proof for approval or the opportunity to request changes. When editor Hannah Morrill got a small watercolor of the family cabin (pictured above), it became a talking point for admiring visitors.

Get inked

Inkbox Custom Tattoos

Available in five sizes, these custom, blue-black tattoos come in a design of your choosing and last up to 10 days.

Buy from Inkbox

Rebels for a week may get a kick out of Inkbox Tattoos, which come in a wide variety of designs. But for particularly artistic types, you can get one of the simple blue-ink temporary tattoos custom-made by either uploading an illustration or typing out text in a font of your choice. They come in six sizes, ranging from small (2 by 2 inches) to extra wide (7 by 2 inches). After the tattoo is applied to the skin for about an hour (so that the ink sinks in), it will last up to 10 days, but it can also be gently rubbed off with an exfoliant if your giftee wants to remove it sooner.

A sense of place

Home Town Map Riedel Crystal Rocks Glass

A map of any location in the US can be etched into this beautiful crystal whiskey glass from storied German glass-maker Riedel.

$35 from Etsy

Your loved one need not be a whiskey drinker to appreciate a Home Town Map Riedel Crystal Rocks Glass. Made with lead-free crystal in Germany, the 13-ounce Riedel glass could certainly hold water, juice, or a prebiotic soda. But more notably, you can have it etched with a map of your chosen US city or town. To select the locale for the engraving, type a name in the Etsy seller’s drop-down menu or use coordinates if the name doesn’t auto-populate. You can also add custom text to the side and bottom.

Sweet tee

Sézane Conrad T-Shirt

This light yet opaque organic-cotton tee has a relaxed, slightly oversize shape and can be personalized with complimentary embroidery, which is a lovely touch.

$40 from Sézane

One of our picks for the best women’s white T-shirts, the Sézane Conrad is made of cool, light 100% organic cotton and has a relaxed crewneck shape. The long, breezy crew is available in sizes XXS to XXXL and several colors beyond white, including green, rosewood, and thin blue stripes. While you can certainly nab a T-shirt for absurd prices these days, the Conrad is eminently reasonable, given its quality material, and adding an embroidered message of up to 10 characters or a symbol (heart or star) comes nicely free of charge.

Engraved bubbly

Williams Sonoma Heart Mini Bottles

Each mini champers split features an etching of the couple’s names, along with their anniversary date, hand-painted in gold.

$127 from Williams Sonoma

If a major milestone, such as an anniversary or an engagement, is the cause for celebration, the Williams Sonoma Heart Mini Bottles will make your giftee’s toasts extra special. When we ordered them, we were impressed by the deeply cut etching in thick glass, with hearts accenting the gold debossed names. Each 6-ounce bottle of bubbly — right-sized for a couple of flutes — is etched with two first names and a date. Offered in packs of six, 12, or 24, the customized bottles are available for six different sparkling wines, including Moët & Chandon Champagne and La Marca prosecco.

Cartoon of them

Happy Tooned

This cartoon service offers fast, accurate caricatures in the style of about a dozen different cartoon and anime shows, including “Bob’s Burgers” and “Dragon Ball Z.”

$40 from Happy Tooned

The same types who would roll their eyes at the sight of monogrammed towels might lose their minds over a Happy Tooned portrait. The service offers custom illustrations in the style of about a dozen popular shows, including The Simpsons and Family Guy. Just choose the number of characters and a background, upload photos of the included people and pets, and note any desired details (particular clothing, poses, and so forth) in a message box. When we tested the service (see above), a mock-up came much faster than the promised 10 days, and requested adjustments were turned around quickly. You can opt for either a digital file or a 12-by-16-inch unframed or framed poster.

Life reflections

Storyworth

This gift-worthy subscription service sends weekly prompts so that your gift recipient can write their own stories, which are then compiled along with personal photos or art into a hardcover book.

Buy from Storyworth

Video manager Katie Quinn reports that Storyworth is the best gift she’s ever given her dad. The service is designed to help someone write their own life story. Weekly email prompts, including questions like “Do you have any regrets in your life?” or “What is one of your fondest childhood memories?” help them recall significant moments. At the end of a year, those answers, along with any personal photos or art, are bound into a quality hardcover book. If your giftee isn’t the type to keep up with email, it may not be the right choice, but it’ll be a joy (and a family keepsake) for those who are both in a stage of reflection and on top of their inbox.

Hometown pride

Hometown Apparel Classic Sweatshirt

This service offers crewneck sweatshirts emblazoned with the name of nearly any town, no matter how small. Custom options are also available.

$40 from Hometown Apparel

Anyone who hails from, lives in, or frequents a small town knows that cool hometown merch is hard to find. But don’t despair: Hometown Apparel produces designs for nearly every town you can think of. Passumpsic, Vermont, population 1,663? Get a shirt. Jemez Springs, New Mexico, with 197 residents? Yep. We’re partial to the simple, collegiate look of the cotton-and-polyester-blend Classic Crewneck Sweatshirt, which comes in four colors and sizes XS to 4XL. If your loved one’s hamlet isn’t there, you can create a custom option using the site’s easy design tool. The sweatshirts run small, so we suggest ordering a size up. Hannah had Long Lake, Maine, sweatshirts made for her family as a homage to a favorite summer spot; four years on, they’re sumptuously soft and faded.

Mini-mes

Funko Pop Yourself

With an addictively fun and easy-to-use module, you can design toys in your loved ones’ likeness, choosing from options for skin tone, hairstyle, clothing, fun accessories, and pets.

Buy from Funko

It doesn’t get more personal than a Funko Pop Yourself figure in one’s image. The website’s easy-to-use module offers a range of choices in skin tone, hairstyles, outfits, accessories, and, for an additional fee, pets. Wirecutter product testing logistics coordinator Leslie Grisdale and her husband were delighted to receive a pair, complete with wine bottle and whisk, from her mother-in-law.

Wrapped around their finger

GoldPersonalized Sterling Silver Name Ring

Small and lightweight, this sterling silver ring can be customized with a name or any meaningful word spelled out in simple cursive.

$25 from Etsy

The dainty Sterling Silver Name Ring by Etsy seller GoldPersonalized is so much nicer than its price suggests. It can be worn alone or stacked with other rings. It comes in sterling silver and yellow, white, or rose gold plate. You can also have it personalized with a name or any meaningful word. A gift box is included with each order, which takes about two weeks to arrive.

Steak and the sizzle

Williams Sonoma Monogrammed Steak Brand

Hand forged in Texas, these mini iron meat brands are the real deal, only smaller.

$60 from Williams Sonoma

These Williams Sonoma Monogrammed Steak Brand are made by a Texas cattle rancher, so you know they’re authentic. They’re the exact same style as the ones used on livestock, but at about a foot long with letters just 1.25 inches high,they’re sized for the backyard cookout, not the open range. Choose from one to three letters for each brand and it comes with a wall mount. Simply heat the iron using hot coals, a gas range, or an electric burner and then leave your charred impression. It will mark meat, leather, and even wood (be careful of fire, please).

Retro slideshow

Image 3D RetroViewer - Create Your Own Reel Viewer

This throwback viewfinder comes with a code to create slide reels of your own photos. The easy online interface is hassle-free, and additional reels are affordable enough to start a new tradition.

$35 from Uncommon Goods

This gift may seem a little gimmicky, but writer Samantha Schoech has one sitting on a shelf in her living room, and every single person who comes over picks it up and looks through the baby pictures of her twins. Each Image 3D RetroViewer comes with an order code for one DIY reel, and it will still show your old reels, should you have any kicking around. It took Samantha about 20 minutes to upload seven photos, edit as desired, and create her own reel. However, it took over two weeks for the reel to arrive, so build in some time if you want to give the reel and viewfinder together. Additional reels can be ordered individually — meaning you could gift new ones for every vacation, birth, or impressive meal.

Modern mixtape

ChainsOnly Custom Audio Scan Keychain

The two-inch stainless steel keychain features a scannable barcode that pulls up a special Spotify song or playlist of your choice.

$11 from Etsy

The world lost something beautiful when everyone lost the ability to send subtle but meaningful messages to each other via obsessed-over mixtapes. Enter the new era of heartfelt song exchange, by way of this Spotify Customizable Playlist Keyring. Simply send in the URL of a song or playlist from Spotify that expresses your feelings, and the company will engrave a code onto a stainless steel keychain that your special giftee can then scan and play on their phone. The tags are slightly smaller than a stick of gum and are available in black, silver, rose or yellow gold.

Bracelet with meaning

AnnMariaJewelry Hammered Handwriting Cuff

A high-res scan of a handwritten message is engraved on either side of a dainty cuff, available in a range of affordable metals.

$104 from Etsy

It can be tough to find the right moment to communicate sentimental thoughts to young adults. The Hammered Handwriting Cuff by Etsy seller AnnMariaJewelry will do the job for you in your own handwriting. Choose from sterling silver or 18-karat rose-gold or yellow-gold plate, and send along a photo of your handwritten message (up to seven words). The company will engrave your message as you’ve written it onto the inner or outer side of the bracelet. The cuff is 6.5 millimeters wide and comes in many lengths; the seller offers measuring instructions to accommodate different wrist sizes.

Tool tote

Carhartt Foundry Series Embroidered Tool Tote

Made of rip-resistant and water-repellent fabric, this rugged six-pocket tool bag accommodates up to two lines of text.

$113 from Personalization Mall

Constructed of poke-, rip-, and waterproof polyester in Carhartt’s signature sienna, the Carhartt Foundry Series Embroidered Tool Tote is a great light-duty carryall for the handyperson you love. (If your gift recipient heads to a worksite every day, they’ll probably want something more rugged.) This bag, outfitted with six interior loops and six exterior pockets, is excellent for home projects. For the two lines of personalization, you could go with traditional monogramming, but if your craftsperson rolls their eyes at formality, a tongue-in-cheek nickname or inside joke might be in order.

Pride-of-place puzzle

Map Marketing Personalized Hometown Puzzle

Enter any US address, and this company creates a 400-piece jigsaw-puzzle map with the address at its center.

$40 from Amazon
$50 from Personalization Mall

Friends brimming with hometown pride or nostalgia might appreciate Map Marketing’s My Hometown Personalized Puzzle. The image on this custom-printed, 400-piece puzzle covers 7.5 miles east–west by 5 miles north–south around a US address. When the puzzle arrives, don’t be alarmed by the box’s generic map image; included inside the box is a folded, full-color map, so puzzle-solvers have something to follow. Note to anyone lucky enough to live near a big body of water: It will have a lot of extra-challenging solid blue.

Egg-cellent produce

World of Jay Design Personalized Vintage Egg Stamp

This vintage-style stamp lets coop owners personalize their eggs before giving them away. Mastering a smudge-free application takes some practice.

$9 from Zazzle

We first realized that chickens were hot when our article on raising chickens went gangbusters back in the early days of the pandemic. If someone on your list is the steward of a brood, World of Jay Design’s Personalized Vintage Egg Stamp, available on Zazzle, will surprise and delight. We like this vintage-style version, which you can customize with your pal’s name or their farm’s moniker. Getting the hang of stamping a spherical object takes a little practice, but once they nail it, they’ll never want to distribute a dozen without the stamps.

A notebook they’ll want to write in

Papier Hardcover Notebook

You can customize this lovely and affordable hardback notebook with details such as monogramming and cover colors.

$26 from Papier

This custom beauty from Papier isn’t one of our picks in our guide to the best notebooks, but it sparks joy in a variety of meaningful ways. The Papier Hardback Notebook offers a two-tone cover (available in several color combos) and monogramming at the top (in multiple colors, fonts, and sizes), so you can craft the right vibe for whoever is on your list. And with its 96 pages of lined, plain, or bullet-dotted paper, they’ll have plenty of creative space to draft the perfect thank-you note, with no ink feathering or page bleed-through.

An embroidered cap

VistaPrint Flexfit 6-Panel Baseball Cap

These well-fitting six-panel caps come in three sizes and offer tons of customization options (colors, fonts, images) in a straightforward interface.

$27 from VistaPrint

Embroidering a baseball cap elevates it from sun shield and dirty-hair disguiser to style statement. Writer Dorie Chevlen has given and received great VistaPrint hats, which are high-quality and surprisingly affordable. Encompassing more than 10 hat colors, nearly 50 choices for embroidery thread, and dozens of fonts and text sizes, the options on VistaPrint’s Flexfit 6-Panel Baseball Cap are almost overwhelming.

Name-dropping napkins

American Stationery DYO Color Beverage Napkins

People who have everything don’t always have cute custom napkins. This 50-pack comes in nine colors and a variety of design and typeface options.

$44 from American Stationary

Hannah first spotted these at a pal’s house in Miami, where a can of LaCroix felt luxurious served atop a conch-shell-and-surname-embossed napkin. Though the website looks a little industrial, American Stationery’s DYO Color Beverage Napkins are notably absorbent, soft, and durable, and customizing them is a cinch. First, select the color of the napkin itself; then, choose the size, color, and font of the text. (Hannah’s friend chose white with orange ink.) Add a relevant “motif,” or clip art, and behold the impact of a simple pack of paper napkins.

The night they’ll never forget

The Night Sky Museum Grade Print

These personalized art prints depict how the cosmos looked at a specific moment in time — for example, the day of a birth.

$49 from The Night Sky

You know the day that matters most to your gift recipient: Maybe it was the day they were born, the day they married you, or even just the day they submitted the 400-page dissertation that earned them a PhD. Whatever it is, the Night Sky Museum Grade Print — depicting the stars aligned exactly as they were at that location on that date — is a beautiful gift for both its celestial image and the emotional resonance. The image is printed on museum-quality paper or stretched canvas with fade-resistant archival ink, and it’s available in several colors, plus a glow-in-the-dark option. You can also include the location, the date, and a personal message for the text underneath the constellation.

Drinkware that’s perfect for family night (or every morning)

Uncommon Goods Personalized Family Mugs

Printed with simple line-and-ink illustrations, these sturdy ceramic mugs offer cute depictions of a loved one’s likeness.

$32 from Uncommon Goods

Your circle of family and friends undoubtedly includes a bunch of characters, so why not immortalize their cartoon likenesses with Uncommon Goods’s Personalized Family Mugs? Think of each mug as a ceramic homage that doubles as a vessel for delivering 15 ounces of one’s beverage of choice. Take advantage of Uncommon Goods’s myriad illustration templates — which span more than 40 characters, plus several skin tones, hair colors, and clothing accent colors — to design the perfect mug for your gift recipient. Reviewers love that this novelty gift is sweetly made and sparks joy with every sip.

Socks with faces

DivvyUp Custom Socks

Have a pet’s or person’s mug digitally printed on these polyester, spandex, and nylon socks. Just remember to size up: Snug socks can create distorted faces.

$24 from Amazon
$24 from Divvy Up

What could be more custom than a pair of socks featuring one’s own face — or, better yet, the face of someone they love? The answer is: not much. And to that end, DivvyUp is one of the easiest services to use. Upload an image of a beloved visage and then select a background color or print. A couple of weeks later, face socks will arrive on your gift recipient’s doorstep. Depending on the intended recipient, consider different mugs. A friend from work? Maybe it’s their particularly hilarious ID photo. A pal from high school? Use the yearbook photo they nearly forgot about. Your doggy co-parent? Fido, obviously. Made from a durable blend of polyester, nylon, and spandex, these socks are stretchy and soft, and they hold up well.

Cuter toting

L.L.Bean Boat and Tote

This timeless structured bag is made from durable, long-lasting canvas, and it’s available in multiple colors, sizes, and handle lengths.

$40 from L.L.Bean
(medium)

The person on your list probably has one or two tote bags already, but they may not have one that’s unequivocally, without a doubt, theirs. For that, give them a tote with a monogram. The L.L.Bean Boat and Tote Open-Top is one of our favorites from testing, thanks to its heavy-duty cotton and tight stitching, which can withstand weights of up to 500 pounds (though we doubt that your gift recipient will ever need to haul that much). In addition to personalizing the bag with your loved one’s initials — or an ironic phrase — you can also customize the size (small, medium, large, or extra large), color (12 options, including deep mulberry and regatta blue), and handle length (regular or long). L.L.Bean now offers a fully monochromatic bag, too, on which the handles and the canvas body match — a particularly modern upgrade from the old-school contrast handles.

A formal pet portrait

Crown & Paw Custom Pet Canvas

This vintage-style portrait offers a dignified depiction of your favorite furry (or non-furry) friend.

$40 from Crown & Paw

For people who put their pets on an emotional pedestal, Crown & Paw’s Custom Pet Canvas portraits can visually elevate an animal’s rank to royalty. Whether the pet is a pig, puppy, chicken, or cat, Crown & Paw can swiftly re-create a 19th-century or Renaissance-era portrait in their likeness. One Wirecutter staffer was deeply impressed with the quality of these prints, noting how well the image captured their dog’s goofiness and brindle coat.

A bracelet with meaning

Danique Jewelry Minimalist Birthstone Bracelet

Available in sterling silver or gold vermeil, these delicate birthstone bracelets can accommodate customization with up to six stones to commemorate important months.

$45 from Etsy

DaniqueJewelry’s Minimalistic Birthstones Bracelets, available on Etsy, are pleasing in their simplicity, featuring a dainty silver or gold-filled chain of varying lengths to fit your gift recipient’s wrist size. Maybe you’ll select just one gem — your recipient’s birthstone, perhaps. Or, if you’re buying for a romantic partner, consider adding both of your birthstones. Dorie has this bracelet with a blue topaz and a moonstone, which represent the birth months of her parents, plus a citrine gem for her three siblings (who, like her, were all born in November). She wears it whenever she needs a simple accessory or wants to feel connected to them.

Fancy photo book

Artifact Uprising Hardcover Photo Book

This stylish book has only a few design options. But the luxe linen cover, matte paper, and decent print quality all make the higher price worth it.

Buy from Artifact Uprising

Artifact Uprising, Wirecutter’s pick for the best photo book for gifting, offers an artful and streamlined way to make a true keepsake. Six trim sizes and only a handful of interior page layouts work as guardrails to ensure that the final product is elegant and legible. The linen-bound book comes in 10 colors, with the option of a dust jacket, and the matte paper is thick and toothy. If you’re looking for more photo-gift inspiration, check out our guide to other personalized photo gifts we love.

Extra-special stationery

Joy Creative Shop Personalized Stationery

These affordable custom note cards are printed on luxuriously thick paper, and you get enough color and design options to create something that feels extra-special.

$28 from Etsy

Epistolary correspondence still beats email and text, and Snaps for grace, style, and posterity. The problem is, custom stationery usually costs a fortune. The letter writer on your list will appreciate the high-quality Joy Creative Shop Classic Monogram Personalized Stationery set (available on Etsy), which lets you select from nine cheery colors for the envelope and 30 hues for the text. The 110-pound card stock has a satisfying heft and a slightly textured feel, and the printing is opaque and consistent, with no blips or errors.

Un-snatchable hydration

Yeti Rambler Tumbler with MagSlider Lid (20 ounces)

A wider profile makes this stainless steel Yeti tumbler feel at home on a farm, at a construction site, or at camp. The MagSlider lid reduces the likelihood of spills, though it’s not leakproof.

$35 from Amazon
$35 from REI
$35 from Yeti

A Wirecutter runner-up for a particularly sturdy tumbler, the 20-ounce Yeti Rambler Tumbler is great. Yeti makes customizations easy: Add text or monogramming in a variety of fonts and colors, pick a design from among Yeti’s collection (including sports logos, Greek letters, and some inoffensive clip art), or upload your own black-and-white image. The customizable area is relatively generous — all the better for adding a meaningful quote, an inside joke, or detailed lost-and-found contact information.

Wild low-tops

Converse Custom Chuck Taylor All Star

Choose colors and prints for almost every part of these affordable, iconic sneakers. You can go a little nuts on the design, selecting leopard print for the canvas, a metallic gold for the eyelets, and any number of other fun variations.

Buy from Converse

One of our picks for the best white sneakers, Converse’s Chuck Taylor All Star low-tops are also especially customizable, allowing you to choose the colors and prints for the inside and outside body, the tongue, the eyelets, the laces, the rubber, and every other part of the shoe. You can also have them embroidered with up to six letters on the outside low rear or on the heel stripe. Initials on the heel stripe are a stylistically safe bet when you are giving a gift.
